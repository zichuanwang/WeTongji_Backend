How does WeTongji get the information of users?Is it safe?

WeTongji gets parts of the information from Academic Affairs of Tongji University each term，and uploads it to the server .Our operations team will affix the Confidentiality agreement and maintain the data under the supervision of the advisor of the Wetongji.

Is the information of activities published via WeTongji reliable?

WeTongji is not only an app for spreading information，but also a platform provider.Any information of activity is published by the school organization ，which is certified ，via the WeTongji Admin System.

How could I deal with the information of activities from the channels?

For the users, you could add the activities both to your favorite and schedule. Even more, you could give a "like".

Will WeTongji develop more new functions？
