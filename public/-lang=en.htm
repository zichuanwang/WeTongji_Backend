    WeTongji 3.0 is coming SOON!!!

    Good news for the users of Windows Phone7~~

    Half year after the release of WeTongji(iOS&Android), the students in Tongji who are using windows phones have kept asking for the windows phone version of WeTongji. Eventually, the developers were involved in the programming for a couple months. And now goes the windows phone7!(Notes:For those who are using windows phone8, all you need to do is wait for a little bit.)

  • Schedule：After logged in, you will see all the courses you take in Tongji automatically, including every detail of the course. And with a sweet alarm 10 minutes before class.
  • Activity：You can see all the event info published by over 100 college organizations. Check it out everyday and never miss any one!
  • News：Integration of the news and notice of the most importance, including college notification, club announcements and the preferential info around campus. Provide you a wonderful campus life!
  • Weekly person：Detailed introduction of a great teacher with a low profile. Get a deep insight of Tongji.
